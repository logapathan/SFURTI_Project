Introduction

The Tirur Coconut Producer Company Ltd. (TCPC), located in Malappuram district of Kerala, is a significant agricultural cooperative focused on coconut farming. It comprises over 22,000 farmers managing more than 5 lakh coconut palms. The TCPC is organized into 180 primary Coconut Producer Societies (CPSs) and 12 Coconut Producer Federations (CPFs), ensuring efficient cooperation and resource utilization. The company has an authorized share capital of Rs. 550 lakhs and a paid-up share capital of Rs. 295 lakhs. The primary aim is to promote value addition and enhance the livelihoods of its members through various initiatives, including the establishment of an integrated processing facility for flavoured coconut milk and virgin coconut oil

Products Made by the Cluster

TCPC focuses on producing flavoured coconut milk and virgin coconut oil. They utilize a variety of specialized machinery for value-added products like tender coconut water, coconut milk, coconut cream, and coconut chips. The company plans to produce and market these products domestically and internationally, enhancing the coconut value chain and increasing farmers' incomes

Infrastructure

The Tirur Coconut Cluster has implemented various soft interventions to enhance productivity and sustainability:
● Friends of Coconut Trees (FOCT): Training 200 trainees for skilled raw nut collection.
● Technical Skill Training: Training 120 beneficiaries to improve the value chain from manufacturing to packaging.
● Exposure Visits and Cluster Twinning: Learning from other coconut processing clusters in Tamil Nadu and Karnataka.
● ERP System for Inventory Management: Modernising operations for efficient inventory tracking.
● Marketing Plan: Developing a comprehensive marketing strategy with branding and e-commerce capabilities.

Workshop and Training Programs

The cluster undertakes various soft interventions to improve productivity and sustainability, including the "Friends of Coconut Trees (FOCT)" program, which trains individuals in coconut collection and processing. They also provide technical skill training, exposure visits, and implement an ERP system for inventory management. Additionally, TCPC collaborates with the National Institute of Micro Small and Medium Enterprises (NIMSME) for research and development support.
